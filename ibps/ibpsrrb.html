IBPS RRB (Gramin Bank) CRP XV: Comprehensive Guide

Regional Rural Banks (RRBs) were established to serve the rural hinterlands of India. The recruitment through IBPS RRB is unique because it removes the English barrier in Prelims and allows candidates to work close to their roots. This guide covers everything from salary slips to the Proficiency Test.

1. SALARY & CAREER HIERARCHY (2026)

Following the 12th Bipartite Wage Settlement, RRB salaries are now at par with nationalized banks.

Office Assistant (Clerk) ₹42,000 - ₹48,000 Gross Monthly Pay
Officer Scale I (PO) ₹75,000 - ₹82,000 Gross Monthly Pay

Career Path: Assistant Manager (Scale I) → Manager (Scale II) → Senior Manager (Scale III) → Chief Manager (Scale IV) → Assistant General Manager → DGM → General Manager.
