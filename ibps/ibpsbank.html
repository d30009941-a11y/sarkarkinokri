IBPS & SBI: PO and Clerk Master Recruitment Guide

This comprehensive guide covers the recruitment process for 11 Public Sector Banks via IBPS and the State Bank of India (SBI). Under the 12th Bipartite Wage Settlement, banking has become one of the most lucrative "white-collar" government careers in India. This roadmap is trusted by aspirants for its deep research into selection trends.

1. SALARY & GROWTH (LATEST STANDARDS)
