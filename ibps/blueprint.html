The Colossal 90-Day Banking & Insurance Blueprint (2026)

Forget random practice. This blueprint is designed using the 70-20-10 Learning Model: 70% Practice, 20% Analysis, 10% Concept. Follow this, and you aren't just taking an exam—you are claiming your seat in the financial elite.

PHASE 1: THE FOUNDATION (DAYS 1-30)

01

Calculation Speed (The Clerical Core)

In Prelims, the difference between success and failure is 5 seconds per question. Master tables up to 30, squares to 50, and cubes to 30.

DAILY TASK 30 Mins of mental math before breakfast.
02

Grammar & Reading Context

Stop memorizing rules. Start reading 'The Hindu' or 'Indian Express' editorials. Your brain must learn to identify Contextual Error Spotting.

DAILY TASK Summarize one editorial in 50 words.

THE 15-MINUTE RETENTION CLUES (EXPERT SECRETS)

  • Clue #1: If a Puzzle takes more than 3 minutes in Prelims—LEAVE IT. You can solve 5 small questions in that time.
  • Clue #2: Descriptive Writing is marked by AI. Use "Transition Words" like Furthermore, Conversely, Consequently to boost scores.
  • Clue #3: For General Awareness, focus on Numbers. Exams are asking specific % rates and ₹ Crore values, not just scheme names.
  • Clue #4: Solve 1 Full Mock every 3 days. Spending 2 hours analyzing the mock is better than taking 5 mocks without analysis.

Your mental stamina is now your biggest asset. Practice sitting for 3 hours continuously to mimic the Mains environment.

🎯 The Revision Loop: Every Sunday, do not touch new topics. Re-solve the difficult questions from your previous week's mocks. This is called Spaced Repetition, the gold standard for long-term memory.
