SBI Junior Associate (Clerk): Master Entry Guide

The SBI Clerk (Junior Associate) role is the backbone of customer operations at the State Bank of India. Unlike the PO cadre, Clerical recruitment is **state-specific**, meaning you must be proficient in the local language of the state you apply for.

1. ELIGIBILITY & AGE RELAXATION

Education Academic Norms: A Degree (Graduation) in any discipline from a recognised University. Candidates in the final year/semester may apply provisionally provided they produce proof of passing by the date specified in the official notification.

Age Entry Window: 20 to 28 years.
• SC/ST: +5 Years | • OBC: +3 Years | • PwBD (Gen): +10 Years

2. PRELIMINARY EXAM (PHASE-I)

This is a screening test. Marks obtained here are not added to the final merit list, but you must pass the sectional cutoff (if applicable) and the aggregate cutoff.

4. STRUCTURAL SYLLABUS BREAKDOWN

Reasoning & Computer

  • Puzzles & Seating Arrangement
  • Syllogism & Coding-Decoding
  • Input-Output & Blood Relations
  • Binary Logic & Flowcharts
  • Networking & OS Basics

Quantitative Aptitude

  • Data Interpretation (Bar/Pie/Line)
  • Simplification/Approximation
  • Number Series & Quadratic Eq.
  • Arithmetic (Profit, SI/CI, Time)
  • Data Sufficiency

General/Financial GA

  • Current Affairs (Last 6 Months)
  • Banking Terminology (SLR, CRR)
  • RBI Functions & Monetary Policy
  • Government Schemes & Budget
  • Headquarters & Static GK

5. THE LOCAL LANGUAGE TEST (LPT)

Is it Mandatory? If you have studied the official local language in Class 10th or 12th (as evidenced by your mark sheet), you are exempt. Others must undergo a proficiency test (Reading, Writing, Speaking). Candidates failing the LPT will not be offered an appointment.

6. SALARY, PROBATION & BENEFITS

  • Pay Scale: The starting basic pay is approximately ₹19,900/- (with two advance increments for graduates).
  • Total Emoluments: Including DA, HRA, and other allowances, the gross salary in a metro city like Mumbai is roughly ₹37,000 - ₹40,000 per month.
  • Probation: Newly recruited Junior Associates will be on probation for a period of 6 months.
