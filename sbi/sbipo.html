SBI Probationary Officer (PO) 2026: Complete Career & Selection Blueprint

Becoming an SBI PO is considered the pinnacle of a banking career in India. This guide provides a 360-degree view of the recruitment process, from basic eligibility to the final interview stage and beyond.

1. ELIGIBILITY & AGE LIMIT

Academic Mandatory Requirement: Graduation in any discipline from a recognized University. Candidates in the final year of graduation may apply provisionally, provided they produce proof of passing the exam on or before the interview date.

Attempts Strict Limits: General/EWS: 4 Attempts | OBC: 7 Attempts | SC/ST: No Limit.
*(Note: Appearing in Prelims is not counted as an attempt. Counting starts from Mains).*

Age Range: 21 to 30 years as of 1st April 2026.
• OBC (Non-Creamy): 33 years | • SC/ST: 35 years | • PwBD: 40-45 years.

This stage is qualifying in nature. Marks will not be added to final merit but passing sectional cutoffs is mandatory.

4. EXHAUSTIVE SYLLABUS BREAKDOWN

🧠 Reasoning Ability

  • Complex Puzzles (Floor, Box, Circular)
  • Machine Input-Output (Advanced)
  • Critical Reasoning (Statement-Assumption)
  • Coded Inequalities & Syllogism
  • Direction Sense & Blood Relations

🔢 Data Analysis & Quant

  • Caselets & Probability
  • Missing & Wrong Number Series
  • Arithmetic (Time, Speed, Profit & Loss)
  • Advanced Data Interpretation (Radar/Funnel)
  • Quadratic Equations & Data Sufficiency

🏦 Banking & Economy

  • RBI Monetary Policy & Repo Rates
  • Union Budget 2026 Highlights
  • Capital & Money Markets
  • Government Social Schemes (PMJJBY, etc.)
  • Current Affairs (International & National)

✍️ English Language

  • Inference-based Reading Comprehension
  • Sentence Connector & Cloze Test
  • Error Detection (Grammar-based)
  • Vocabulary & Idioms
  • Descriptive Essay on Current Issues

5. SALARY, PERKS & PROMOTION

Starting Basic Pay: ₹41,960/- (with 4 advance increments).
In-hand Salary: Approx. ₹65,000 - ₹70,000 per month depending on the city of posting.

Leased Accommodation (Up to ₹35k) Petrol Allowance (50-60 Liters) Medical Insurance (100% for Self) LTC Facility Smart Phone/Book Grant

Career Growth: PO → Manager → Senior Manager → Chief Manager → AGM → DGM → GM → CGM → MD & CEO.

6. 90-DAY PREPARATION ROADMAP

Days 1-30: Concept Foundation

Focus on clearing basic arithmetic and grammar rules. Practice 50+ Simplification and 20+ Puzzles daily.

Days 31-60: Speed & Accuracy

Start taking sectional mocks. Focus on increasing speed in Quantitative Aptitude and accuracy in English.

Days 61-90: Full Length Mocks & GA

Take one full-length mock every day. Dedicate 2 hours daily to Current Affairs and Banking Awareness notes.

7. SELECTION RATIO & CUTOFF TRENDS

SBI PO is highly competitive. Usually, only 1-2% of total applicants make it to the final list. Cutoffs for Prelims generally range between 58-63 (General), while Mains cutoffs vary significantly based on paper difficulty.
