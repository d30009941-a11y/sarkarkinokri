4. SARKARKINOKRI "TRUSTED ADVISOR" INSIGHTS

  • Merit Calculation: Final merit is prepared based only on the 390 marks of Section-I and Section-II of Tier-II. Tier-I is only a gate-pass.
  • The Computer Hurdle: Even with a 350+ score, if you fail the qualifying Computer module, you will not be considered for any post.
  • DEST Note: Typing is now mandatory for all posts, but the "Error Percentage" allowed is much lower for Tax Assistant posts.
