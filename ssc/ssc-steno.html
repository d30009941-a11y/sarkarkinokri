SSC Stenographer Grade 'C' & 'D': Official Guide

1. THE UNIQUE EXAM PATTERN (NO MATHEMATICS)

SSC Stenographer is a specialized exam focusing on Language and Reasoning. There is no Mathematics paper in this recruitment.

Note: There is a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer.

2. STENOGRAPHY SKILL TEST (SHORTHAND)

Candidates qualified in the CBT will appear for the Skill Test. You will be given one dictation for 10 minutes in English or Hindi.

4. DETAILED SYLLABUS

🔤 English Language & Comprehension (100 Marks)

This is the most critical section. It tests Vocabulary, Grammar, Sentence Structure, Synonyms, Antonyms, and correct usage. Includes writing ability, error recognition, and fill in the blanks.

🧠 General Intelligence & Reasoning (50 Marks)

Includes both verbal and non-verbal types. Analogies, similarities and differences, space visualization, problem solving, analysis, judgment, decision making, visual memory, and arithmetical reasoning.

🌍 General Awareness (50 Marks)

Current events and matters of everyday observation. Questions related to India and its neighboring countries, History, Culture, Geography, Economic scene, General Polity, and Scientific Research.
