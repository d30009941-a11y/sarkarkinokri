SSC Preparation Blueprint: The 90-Day Success Path

Success in SSC CGL, CHSL, or MTS isn't about studying 15 hours a day; it's about studying the right topics in the right order. Below is our expert-verified roadmap.

🚀 Phase 1: The Foundation (Day 1 - 30)

Focus on clearing concepts. Do not touch mock tests yet. Master the basics of Arithmetic and English Grammar rules.

TARGET: Complete 60% of the syllabus & hand-written notes.
⚙️ Phase 2: Speed & Accuracy (Day 31 - 60)

Start solving Chapter-wise Previous Year Questions (PYQs). Focus on short-tricks for Geometry and Algebra. Build reading speed for English Comprehension.

TARGET: Solve 50 questions per subject daily.
🏆 Phase 3: The Mock Marathon (Day 61 - 90)

One Full-Length Mock Test every morning. Analyze the mistakes for 3 hours in the evening. This phase decides your rank.

TARGET: Improve score by 5-10 marks every week.
