🔍 The "Invisible" Allowances Breakdown

Official PDF data often misses these critical salary components:

1. National Holiday Allowance (NHA)

Working on a holiday like Oct 2nd or Jan 26th grants you a fixed daily rate. Level 1-2: ₹384 | Level 6: ₹630 per day.

2. Night Duty Allowance (NDA)

Calculated per hour for work between 22:00 and 06:00. For Station Masters, this adds up to ₹3,500/month based on shifts.

3. Dress & Festival Allowance

Trackmen/ALP receive ₹5,000 yearly for uniforms. Plus, a ₹7,000 Productivity Linked Bonus (PLB) is paid annually before Diwali.
