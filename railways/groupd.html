RRB GROUP D (LEVEL-1) RECRUITMENT 2026

CENTRALIZED EMPLOYMENT NOTICE (CEN) NO. RRC-01/2026

Mentor's Personal Advice: Group D has the highest number of vacancies but also the highest competition. Success here isn't about solving "hard" questions; it's about solving 100 easy questions without a single mistake in 90 minutes. Physical fitness is 50% of the selection. Start walking 4km daily from today.
1. Eligibility & Age (पात्रता और आयु सीमा)
Academic Qualification 10th Pass (High School) OR ITI from institutions recognized by NCVT/SCVT OR National Apprenticeship Certificate (NAC).
Age Limit 18 to 33 Years (General). Relaxations: OBC (3 yrs), SC/ST (5 yrs).
Medical Category Standard A-2, A-3, B-1, B-2, C-1 (Post-wise). Visual acuity is strictly checked.
2. Examination Scheme (CBT Pattern)

The Computer Based Test (CBT) will be of 90 minutes duration for 100 questions. There will be Negative Marking (1/3rd) for every wrong answer.

3. Physical Efficiency Test (PET) - Mandatory

Candidates equal to 3 times the vacancies will be called for PET. This is qualifying in nature but mandatory for final selection.

For Male Candidates

  • Lift & Carry 35 kg of weight for 100 meters in 2 minutes (without putting down).
  • Run 1000 meters (1 km) in 4 minutes and 15 seconds in one chance.

For Female Candidates

  • Lift & Carry 20 kg of weight for 100 meters in 2 minutes (without putting down).
  • Run 1000 meters (1 km) in 5 minutes and 40 seconds in one chance.
4. Detailed Syllabus (विषय-वार पाठ्यक्रम)
Mathematics: Number system, BODMAS, Decimals, Fractions, LCM, HCF, Ratio and Proportion, Percentages, Mensuration, Time and Work, Time and Distance, Simple and Compound Interest, Profit and Loss, Algebra, Geometry and Trigonometry, Elementary Statistics, Square root, Age Calculations, Calendar & Clock, Pipes & Cistern.
General Science: The syllabus under this shall cover Physics, Chemistry and Life Sciences of 10th standard level (CBERT/NCERT). Focus on light, electricity, chemical reactions, and human anatomy.
5. Application & Important Documents
DANGER: Do not upload blurred photos. Ensure the name and date are printed on the photo if requested by the RRB. Use a White Background only.

Documents to keep ready for Digital Upload:

  • Scanned Passport Photo (JPEG: 20kb to 50kb).
  • Scanned Signature (JPEG: 10kb to 40kb).
  • SC/ST Certificate (for free travel pass request).

Documents for DV (Physical Verification): 10th Certificate, ITI/Apprenticeship Certificate, Caste Certificate (Central Format), Aadhaar Card (both sides), and 12 copies of the photo used in the application.
