Official Syllabus 2026

CEN Official Topic Mapping: ALP, Tech, NTPC & Group D

1. Mathematics (CBT-1 & CBT-2)

Standard: 10th Class Level (Arithmetic & Elementary Algebra)

  • Number System, BODMAS, Decimals, Fractions CEN 01/2024
  • LCM & HCF, Ratio and Proportion, Percentages Official
  • Mensuration (2D/3D), Time and Work, Time and Distance Official
  • Simple and Compound Interest, Profit and Loss Official
  • Elementary Algebra, Geometry and Trigonometry Advanced
  • Elementary Statistics, Square Root, Age Calculations Official
  • Calendar & Clock, Pipes & Cistern Reasoning-Mixed

2. General Intelligence & Reasoning

Focus: Logical and Analytical Ability

  • Analogies, Alphabetical and Number Series Core
  • Coding and Decoding, Mathematical operations Core
  • Relationships (Blood Relations), Syllogism Analytical
  • Jumbling, Venn Diagram, Data Interpretation Data-Heavy
  • Conclusions and Decision making Critical
  • Similarities and Differences, Analytical reasoning Official
  • Classification, Directions, Statement-Arguments Official

4. General Awareness (Current Affairs)

  • Science & Technology (Space/Nuclear Programs) High Weight
  • Sports (National & International) Official
  • Culture, Personalities, Economics, Politics Official
  • Indian History & Freedom Struggle Static
  • Physical, Social and Economic Geography Static
  • Environmental Issues (India & World) New Trend

🏆 The "CBT-2" Technical Shift

For **Technician Grade 1 (Signal)**, the syllabus expands to include **Basics of Computers and Applications** (Architecture, Networking, Operating Systems) and **Basic Science & Engineering** (Electricity & Magnetism). Do not confuse this with the standard Group D syllabus.
