Medical Standards 2026

Official RRB Visual & Physical Matrix

Based on the Indian Railway Medical Manual (IRMM). Standards for CEN 2024-2026.

🔴 IMPORTANT: Railway Medical is 10X stricter than SSC or Police. A candidate with "Color Blindness" or "LASIK" in safety categories (A1/A2/A3) is disqualified instantly without any alternative post-allocation.

1. Post-Wise Visual Standards

Category Posts (Example) Distant Vision Near Vision LASIK Policy
A-1 ALP (Loco Pilot) 6/6, 6/6 (No Glass) Sn 0.6, 0.6 NOT ALLOWED
A-2 Station Master, Guard, Pointsman 6/9, 6/9 (No Glass) Sn 0.6, 0.6 NOT ALLOWED
A-3 Shunting Staff, Loco Dept 6/9, 6/9 (With/Without) Sn 0.6, 0.6 NOT ALLOWED
B-1 Signal Tech, JE, Staff Nurse 6/9, 6/12 (With Glass) Sn 0.6, 0.6 ALLOWED*
B-2 / C-1 Clerks, Accountants, Staff 6/12, 6/18 (With Glass) Sn 0.6 (Reading) ALLOWED

2. Specialized Mandatory Tests

Fogging Test (Only for A-1)

Doctors use a +2D lens to test if you are hiding hypermetropia. If you can read the 6/12 line while "fogged," you are unfit. This is the #1 reason for ALP rejection.

Ishihara Color Test

Used to detect Color Blindness. Required for A-1, A-2, A-3, and B-1. If you cannot differentiate red/green signals, you cannot work in safety roles.

3. General Physical Fitness

Condition Standard / Requirement Status
Hearing Audiometry test to check if you can hear frequencies between 250-8000 Hz. Mandatory
Diabetes HbA1c must be under 6.5. Chronic high sugar leads to temporary unfitness. Mandatory
Blood Pressure Standard 140/90. High BP is the #2 reason for "Temporary Unfitness." Mandatory
Flat Foot / Knock Knee Checked, but usually not a reason for rejection in Railway (unlike Defense). Relaxed

🏆 Mentor's Final Checklist

  1. If you have had LASIK, do not apply for ALP/Station Master. You will be caught by the slit-lamp test.
  2. Check your BMI. While there is no "weight" limit, morbid obesity causes high BP/Sugar, leading to medical failure.
  3. Carry your previous prescription if you wear glasses for B-1 or C-1 posts.
