90-Day Battle Plan: From Aspirant to Railway Employee

Mentor's Strategy: Most students fail because they study everything but master nothing. This blueprint follows the 70-20-10 Rule: 70% time on high-weightage topics, 20% on weak areas, and 10% on daily current affairs. Stick to the phases.

PHASE 1: Concept Mastery

Day 1 - Day 30
The "No-Mock" Zone

Do not waste time on full-length mocks yet. Focus on clearing your concepts for Math and Science.

  • Mathematics: Complete Number System, Percentage, and Ratio (These form the base for 60% of Math).
  • Science: Finish NCERT Class 9th & 10th Physics chapters (Light, Electricity, Motion).
  • Reasoning: Master Syllogism and Coding-Decoding puzzles.
  • Daily: Read 30 minutes of Current Affairs (Focus on National News & Sports).

PHASE 2: The Drill Phase

Day 31 - Day 60
Sectional Timers

This phase is about reducing your "Time Per Question." You must aim for < 45 seconds per question.

  • Sectional Tests: Give 1 sectional test for Math and Reasoning every alternate day.
  • Science Revision: Start solving Previous Year Questions (PYQs) from 2018-2024 exams.
  • Elimination Tech: Learn "Option Elimination" for General Awareness.
  • Static GK: Memorize Indian Railways history, Dams, Rivers, and Appointments.

PHASE 3: Final Simulation

Day 61 - Day 90
Exam-Mode Enabled

Simulate the actual exam environment. No phones, no breaks, strict 90-minute sessions.

  • Full Mocks: 1 Full-Length Mock every morning. Spend 2 hours analyzing every wrong answer.
  • Shift Sync: Take your mock at the same time as your actual exam shift (Morning/Afternoon/Evening).
  • Formula Cheat-Sheet: Revise all Math and Physics formulas in the last 1 hour of the day.
  • Current Affairs: 1-year Revision Marathon (Jan 2025 to Exam Date).

⚠️ The 48-Hour Safety Net

Two days before the exam: Stop Studying. Your brain needs rest to perform at peak cognitive speed. Just check your Admit Card, ID Proof, and reaching the center location.
