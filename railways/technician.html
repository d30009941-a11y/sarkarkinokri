RRB Technician 2026: Expert Selection Blueprint

1. The Eligibility Pillar: Two Entry Points

You must decide between Level 5 (High Responsibility) and Level 2 (Trade Focus). Note that you can apply for both, but requires separate applications and fees.

Grade I (Signal)

Pay Level 5 | 18-33 Years

B.Sc (Physics/Electronics/CS/IT) OR 3-Year Diploma/B.E./B.Tech in Engineering. Focus is on Signaling systems.

Grade III (Workshop)

Pay Level 2 | 18-30 Years

Matriculation + ITI (NCVT/SCVT) in relevant trades OR 10+2 with Physics & Math (S&T posts only).

Medical Standard B-1: Physical fitness is mandatory. For Grade I (Signal), you need 6/9, 6/12 vision with or without glasses (power ≤ 4D). You must pass tests for Color Vision, Night Vision, and Binocular Vision.

2. Selection Process & Exam Pattern

The 2026 cycle utilizes a **Single-Stage CBT**. There is no "Mains" or "Psycho" test for Technician posts, making the single exam highly competitive.

Grade I Pattern (Total 100 Qns | 90 Mins):
  • Basic Science & Engineering: 35 Marks
  • Basics of Computers & Applications: 20 Marks
  • Mathematics (Standard): 20 Marks
  • General Intelligence & Reasoning: 15 Marks
  • General Awareness: 10 Marks
Grade III Pattern (Total 100 Qns | 90 Mins):
  • General Science (Physics/Chem/Bio): 40 Marks
  • Mathematics: 25 Marks
  • General Intelligence & Reasoning: 25 Marks
  • General Awareness: 10 Marks

Mentor Tip: Grade III is 40% Science. Use 10th Standard NCERT as your Bible.

3. Surgical Topic-Wise Syllabus

Grade I (Signal) High-Yield

Computers: Networking, OS (Windows/Unix/Linux), MS Office, Web Browsers, Computer Architecture.

Advanced Science: Electric Charge, Field & Potential, Ohm’s Law, Magnetic Force, Electronic Devices, and Transducers.

Grade III High-Yield

Math: BODMAS, LCM/HCF, Ratio, Mensuration, Profit & Loss, Square Root, Pipes & Cistern.

Reasoning: Syllogism, Venn Diagrams, Data Interpretation, Blood Relations, Coding-Decoding.

4. How to Apply & Document Protocol

Mentor Strategic Tip: Your RRB Zone choice is final. Check the vacancy ratio for your specific ITI trade (Fitter/Electrician/Welder) in each zone before submitting.
  • Step 1: Register on official RRB portal with Aadhaar-linked mobile.
  • Step 2: Upload Photo (White BG, 35x45mm) and Signature (Blue/Black ink).
  • Step 3: Select Grade Level and Post Preferences.
  • Step 4: Fee Payment (₹500 for Gen, ₹250 for SC/ST/Female - refundable after CBT).

Documents Required for DV (Keep Ready)

  • 10th/SSLC Marksheet (Proof of Birth).
  • NCVT/SCVT ITI Certificate OR Engineering Degree/Diploma.
  • Community Certificate (Central Format for OBC/SC/ST).
  • EBC/EWS Certificate (if claiming fee relaxation).
