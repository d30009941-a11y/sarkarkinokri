RRB JE 2026: The Master-Class for Junior Engineers

🛡️ Mentor Technical Briefing

As an engineer, your biggest trap is "The Technical Bias." Thousands fail RRB JE because they focus 100% on their core branch while ignoring the CBT-1 Non-Tech Filter. Remember: If you don't clear the non-tech screening, your technical expertise will never even be tested. Treat CBT-1 as a speed race and CBT-2 as a precision battle.

1. Eligibility & Educational Vertical

RRB JE is open to both Diploma and Degree holders. Unlike other exams, there is no "experience" required, making it the most coveted entry-level engineering job in India.

Academic Requirements

Three years Diploma OR B.E./B.Tech in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, CS, or IT from a recognized University/Institute.

Age & Relaxation

18 - 33 Years. Standard relaxations apply for OBC (3 years), SC/ST (5 years), and PwBD candidates.

2. Selection Architecture (The Two-Tier System)

The merit is calculated ONLY on the basis of CBT-2 marks. However, CBT-1 is a brutal elimination round where only 15 times the number of vacancies are called for the next stage.

STAGE 1: SCREENING
CBT-1 Pattern (100 Qns | 90 Mins)
  • Math: 30 Marks
  • Reasoning: 25 Marks
  • General Science: 30 Marks
  • General Awareness: 15 Marks
STAGE 2: MERIT
CBT-2 Pattern (150 Qns | 120 Mins)
  • Core Technical Abilities: 100 Marks
  • Physics & Chemistry: 15 Marks
  • Basics of Computers: 10 Marks
  • Basics of Env. & Pollution: 10 Marks
  • General Awareness: 15 Marks
⚡ MENTOR TIP: In CBT-2, a Virtual Calculator will be provided on the screen. Do not rely on physical calculators during practice; master the mouse-click calculation early!

3. Technical Syllabus & "High-Yield" Topics

Civil & Allied

Surveying, Strength of Materials, Building Materials, Concrete Structures, Steel Structures, RCC Design.

Electrical & Allied

Circuit Law, Magnetic Circuits, AC Fundamentals, Electrical Machines, Generation, Transmission & Distribution.

Mechanical & Allied

Thermodynamics, IC Engines, Material Science, Machining & Machine Tools, Fluid Mechanics.

IT & CS

PC Software, Computer Organization, OS, C Language, DBMS, Web Tech & Programming.

4. Application Protocol & Document Vault

Zone Selection Research: As a Junior Engineer, your work environment (Workshop vs. Open Line) depends heavily on the RRB Zone and Department you choose. Analyze the "Technical Post" vacancies before applying.

  • Phase 1: Digital Registration and Aadhaar-linkage.
  • Phase 2: Preference for JE (Regular), JE (IT), or DMS (Depot Material Superintendent).
  • Phase 3: Upload Semester-wise Marksheets to verify aggregate percentage.

Documents for Verification (DV)

Original Degree/Diploma, Final Year Marksheets, Recognition proof of the Institute (AICTE/UGC), Caste Certificate (Central format), and NOC if currently working in any PSU or Govt Dept.
