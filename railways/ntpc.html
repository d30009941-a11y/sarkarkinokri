RRB NTPC 2026: Expert Mentorship & Selection Protocol

1. The Eligibility Gateway

Mentor Insight: Eligibility is more than just age; it is about "Pay Level" alignment. Check if you are Graduate or Undergraduate (12th Pass) before looking at the age limit.
Academic Requirements

Undergraduate Posts: 12th (+2 Stage) or equivalent with minimum 50% marks.

Graduate Posts: University degree or equivalent from a recognized institution.

Age Limit (Relaxation as per Rules)

UG: 18 - 30 Years

Graduate: 18 - 33 Years

Critical Medical Standard: Station Master (A-2) requires 6/9 vision without glasses. No Lasik permitted. Clerical roles (C-1) allow glasses.

2. Tiered Exam Pattern (CBT-1 & CBT-2)

Your success is built on understanding the weightage shift between the screening and merit rounds.

CBT-1 (Screening Round)

90 Minutes | 100 Questions

  • Math: 30 Marks
  • Reasoning: 30 Marks
  • General Awareness: 40 Marks
CBT-2 (Merit Round)

90 Minutes | 120 Questions

  • Math: 35 Marks
  • Reasoning: 35 Marks
  • General Awareness: 50 Marks

3. Topic-Wise Syllabus: The "Winning Zone"

Mathematics

Number System, Decimals, Fractions, LCM/HCF, Ratio, Percentage, Mensuration, Time & Work, Time & Distance, Simple & Compound Interest, Profit & Loss, Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry.

General Intelligence

Analogies, Number Series, Coding-Decoding, Mathematical Operations, Similarities & Differences, Relationships, Analytical Reasoning, Syllogism, Jumbling, Venn Diagrams, Data Interpretation.

General Awareness

Current Events, Games & Sports, Indian Art & Culture, Indian Literature, Monuments & Places, General Science, History of India, Geography, Economics, Environmental Issues, Basics of Computers.

4. Application Protocol & Documentation

  • 1 Registration: Visit the official RRB website and select your RRB (Zone selection is final).
  • 2 Personal Details: Fill in Aadhaar number, Parent's names, and 10th/12th Marksheet details exactly as written.
  • 3 Uploads: JPEG format Photo (35mm x 45mm) and Signature.
  • 4 Payment: Fee varies for General (₹500) and SC/ST/Ex-SM/Female (₹250), with refunds after CBT-1.
Required Documents for DV: 10th/12th Certificate, Degree/Diploma, Category Certificate (OBC-NCL/SC/ST/EWS), Aadhaar Card, Discharge Certificate (for Ex-SM).
